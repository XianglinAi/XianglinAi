Sponsor Welcome

Once you login with your given username (usually your school/ business email) and your password, you will then be directed to a ‘Sponsor Welcome’ page. At the top of the page you may see a short message, depending on the course coordinator's setting, which usually includes a video link to the following page

Within this page, you will be expected to see the followings:

Weekly Sponsor Update

In the weekly sponsor update section, you will be seeing things that will be happening within that week. e.g. currently is non-teaching week so the welcome section will be expected as follow.
This section is set for providing guidance and updating you as sponsors of the current course and project team progress.

My Teams for 20XX Sem X

Your allocated project team will be listed under this section with their details:

  • Teams Letter
  • Team Name
  • Project Allocated for this team
  • POC Email
  • Team Members


With this being showed, it is expected that project teams will be contacting you at the end of Week 3, hence they can inform themselves formally.

Your Project Proposals

This section is where members propose potential projects for consideration in specific tranches. You will be seeing a list of proposed project(s) with the followings:

  • Project Name
  • Status (taken or available)
  • Brief backgrounds


Caution！！！
You as sponsors are able to view and edit these proposals, yet, please DO NOT change old/historic proposals, this is only meant to help updating/tweaking proposals for the current semester.

Thank You Section

This section provides updates for sponsors participating in the Capstone Project for the current semester. It includes information on the program, access to the detailed timetable (with link), and sponsor pack (with link), and an introduction to the CAS (Capstone Assessment System) through a brief video.
You are also more than welcome to change your password if desired through Account Settings.

Project Status

This section is a new feature of CAS, where teams can complete a simple RAG report including:

  • Team
  • Status of Week
  • Scope Clarity
  • Timely Delivery
  • Quality Delivery
  • Product Delivery
  • Status Summary

New Project Idea

This section is for you to add any new project ideas that you would like our fellow students to undertake commencing in Semester. When you tab on the “Add Project Proposal” , it will redirect you to the page looks like the followings. So please fill-in the form providing under this tab if there is any new project

Allocated Project Teams

When a Project Team(s) for 2024 Sem1 is allocated you will find the details here under this section with a “My Team” Tab, redirecting you to another page showing team details.

Project Proposal in Need of a Sponsor

Here is where you will be able to see project(s) that are in need of a sponsor, where you can view the project details and choose the one (s) you want to provide guidance to.

My Teams

Under this page, you will be expected to see your allocated team, it may be one or more with the following team details:

  • Project Name
  • Team Letter
  • Team Name
  • Mode
  • Team Details
    • Team Members’ Name
    • Preferred Name
    • Mode
    • Gender
    • Email
